Private Company IT Access Policy 2026

1. VPN Access
Employees must use company VPN for remote internal access.
VPN password resets are done through IT Helpdesk after identity verification.

2. MFA
All employee accounts must have multi-factor authentication enabled.
If an MFA device is lost, report to IT Helpdesk immediately.

3. Password Policy
Passwords must be at least 12 characters and rotated every 90 days.
Do not share credentials with anyone.

4. Support
For account lockouts or system access issues, contact IT Helpdesk.
