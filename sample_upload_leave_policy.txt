Private Company Leave Policy 2026

1. Leave Request Process
Employees must submit leave requests at least 3 working days before the requested leave date.
The request should be approved by the direct manager in the HR portal.

2. Sick Leave
Employees must inform their manager as soon as possible if they cannot attend work due to illness.
A medical certificate may be required for leave longer than 2 consecutive days.

3. Casual Leave
Employees may use casual leave for short personal matters.
Casual leave should not be planned on peak business days unless approved.

4. Contact
For leave-related questions, contact the HR department or your reporting manager.
